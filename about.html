ABOUT 🐔 J.N LIVESTOCK FARMING LTD.

We are a trusted local poultry and fish farm with other live animals and fresh meats, dedicated to providing high-quality, healthy livestock to families, restaurants, and food vendors.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, our farm started with just 100 chickens and a dream. Today, we proudly supply fresh catfish and well-bred chickens to hundreds of satisfied customers across the region.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To raise healthy, affordable livestock for every home.

Vision: To become one of Nigeria's most reliable sources of fresh poultry and catfish including other live animals and Fresh meats.
